Overview

In the distance future of 2020, congressional representatives duke it out to pass legislature. Money is power, do whatever you can to collect funds: grab small donations, pander to your base, cozy up to lobbyists, or extort the competition. Congress is now in session.

Filibusters is a 2D, side-scrolling, arena, shooter, collect-athon game featuring light-hearted political satire and tons of fun!
